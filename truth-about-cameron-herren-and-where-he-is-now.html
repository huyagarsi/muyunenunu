How Old Is Cameron Herren?

Cameron Herren is 24 years old as of 2023. He was born Cameron Coyle Herren on the 9th day of September 1999 in his home state of Texas, United States of America. Herren was raised by his parents, Chris Herren and Cheryl Herren, alongside his older brother Tristan Herren. His father, Chris, is a filmmaker and editor, while his mother, Cheryl, works as the vice president at State Farm Insurance.

Herren holds American citizenship by birth and belongs to the Caucasian ethnic group. He attended a local elementary school in Texas and Tampa Catholic High School for his education. After that, he proceeded to the prestigious Texas Tech University, where he pursued his bachelor’s degree.

During his days in college, Cameron Herren was known for his passion for car racing. This same passion would lead to the accident, which totally changed his life.

Cameron Herren’s Story

On the 23rd day of May 2018, Cameron Herren, with his brother Tristan as a passenger, was racing one of his pals named, Alexander Barrineau. While this street race was going on, Herren accidentally hit a woman named Jessica Reisinger and her 21-month-old baby, Lillia Reisinger. Jessica, who was pushing her baby daughter in a stroller on the street of Bayshore Boulevard, was trying to cross the street when she observed two cars approaching them at a very high speed.

To save her baby, she quickly pushed the stroller over to the other part of the road. Unfortunately, the stroller did not go far enough, and Cameron’s Ford Mustang vehicle collided with her and her baby. Jessica died on the spot, while her daughter Lillia died the following day, just three months before her second birthday.

Cameron Herren was 18 years old at the time of this accident and was arrested alongside his brother and John Barrineau. Several days later, the three appeared in front of a Temple court, and the trial was scheduled for December 2020. The prosecutors argued that Cameron, 18, and his brother Tristan, 20, were racing another motorist John Barrineau, 17, at a speed exceeding 162 mph.

Cameron Herren was Sentenced to a 24-Year Jail Term in April 2021

John Barrineau pleaded guilty to unauthorized racing and vehicular murder and reached a plea bargain with the state. He was sentenced to six years of prison and 15 years of supervised release. On his own part, Cameron Herren faced a 30-year jail term and opted for an open plea, leaving his fate in the hands of the court.

Cameron Herren’s trial took three years, and a decision was passed down by the courts in April 2021. Hillsborough Circuit Judge Christopher Nash found Cameron guilty of two counts of vehicular homicide. He sentenced him to nine years of imprisonment for the first count and 15 years sentence for the second count.

Although Cameron Herren pleaded for a reduced sentence, the court, unfortunately, upheld 24 years of jail term. The sentence began running in April 2021. Meanwhile, during the sentencing hearing, David Raubenolt, Jessica’s husband and baby Lillia’s father, spoke directly to Cameron Harren and informed the young man that it was critical for him to understand that he had created “everlasting pain and depths of sorrow”.

Cameron Herren, currently 24 years old, is serving his 24 years jail term, which started counting in April 2021. He is serving his sentence at the Graceville Correctional Institution, a secure governmental prison for men in Graceville, Florida, United States of America.
