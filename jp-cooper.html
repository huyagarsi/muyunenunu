JP Cooper

Publish date: 2024-06-01

Cooper in 2018, melis

Birth Name: John-Paul Frederick Cooper

Place of Birth: Manchester, Lancashire, England, U.K.

Date of Birth: 2 November, 1983

Ethnicity: English, possibly one eighth Italian, and other

JP Cooper is an English singer and songwriter.

His mother, Patricia, left the family when he was young. He was raised by his father, Paul Cooper.

JP’s paternal grandfather was named Harold Cooper (whose mother’s maiden surname likely was Heesom). Harold was born in Warrington, Lancashire, England.

JP’s paternal grandmother was Bessie Thomason (the daughter of Alexander Thomason and Ellen Sharples). Bessie was born in Warrington, Lancashire, England. Alexander was the son of a man surnamed Thomason, and of Fanny. Ellen was the daughter of William Sharples and Hannah Sisson.

JP’s maternal grandfather was Frederick Crolla (the son of Anthony Crolla and Sarah Ellen Ball). Frederick was born in Manchester, Lancashire, England. Anthony may have been of Italian descent. The Crolla family is from the Province of Frosinone in Lazio, Italy. Sarah was the daughter of John William Ball and Mary Ellen Burns.

JP’s maternal grandmother was Mary P. Hutton (the daughter of Charles T. Hutton and Ada Dyson). Mary was born in Manchester North, Lancashire, England.
