Fergie's &quot;L.A.Love (la la)&quot; Music Video Features Bikini Body, Sick Abs

Publish date: 2024-05-24

The Dutchess is back! For the first time in eight years, Fergie has released new solo music, and it doesn’t disappoint. The pop star, 39, debuted the music video for her new single “L.A. Love (la la)” on Monday, Nov. 10, and it has all the makings of Fergalicious greatness, complete with her funky signature style and flair. 

Starting off the video in a tiny seafoam green bikini, Josh Duhamel’s wife, who gave birth to their son Axl in August 2013, looked incredible, flaunting her abs in the swimsuit and several other outfits throughout the course of the clip. 

Dancing on a party bus with a giant skull on the front, Fergie broke it down with her fellow dancers. Sprinkled throughout the performance were appearances from celebrities including Chelsea Handler,Wilmer Valderrama, Tommy Chong, and Kelly Osbourne.
