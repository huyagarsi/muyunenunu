Who Is The Richest Cast Member From 'That '70s Show' Today?

Publish date: 2024-07-11

That '70s Show is a cult classic series that is loved by many fans still to this day. Although it took place in the late '90s/early '00s, the show perfectly captured the fashion and life in that decade. That '70s Show was "a comedy revolving around a group of teenage friends, their mishaps, and their coming of age, set in 1970s Wisconsin," according to IMDb.

The cast of the sitcom all skyrocketed to fame during that time, and most of them are still in the entertainment business today. When they first started on the show, the cast were nobodys and were barely getting by on their paychecks. Today, their net worths have changed drastically since the finale in 2006.

Even though they all have had pretty successful careers, some made out better than others. So, who is the richest cast member from That '70s Show? It may surprise you.

10 Don Stark

Don Stark played Bob Pinciotti, Donna's father, on That '70s Show, which would be his most famous role. However, he made his on-screen TV debut on General Hospital. His other credits include Star Trek: First Contact, John Carter, Hit The Floor and other roles. Stark produced several episodes of Friends from 1998 to 2000. Celebrity Net Worth puts his current net worth at $5 million.

9 Debra Jo Rupp

Debra Jo Rupp starred as Kitty, Eric's mother, on the show. That '70s Show is just another worthy credit on her list of roles including Friends, Big, Teacher's Pet, Garfield and many more. She has also dabbled in stage work, which only added to her net worth. As of this year, the internet has her estimated worth from $5 to $6 million.

8 Danny Masterson

Danny Masterson played Stephen Hyde on the show. However, That '70s Show was not his first role, starring in more minor roles before breaking out as his role of Hyde. His other acting credits include The Ranch, Men At Work, Beethoven's 2nd, Yes Man and more. Despite his active acting career and being a DJ on the side, Masterson is only worth around $8 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

7 Kurtwood Smith

Kurtwood Smith played Red Forman, Eric's grumpy dad, on That '70s Show. Besides that role, Smith has made a pretty good career for himself. His acting credits include RoboCop, Rambo 3, The Ranch, Regular Show and more. Smith is estimated to be worth around $10 to $12 million, according to numerous sources. Even though he has had a lot more acting credits and time in the industry, Smith is not the richest actor from the series.

6 Laura Prepon

Laura Prepon played Donna Pinciotti, Eric Forman's neighbor and girlfriend, on the Fox sitcom. Besides That '70s Show, Prepon has some acting credits under her belt including The Girl on the Train, Are You There, Chelsea?, The Kitchen and more, but is most known for her role in Orange is the New Black. Celebrity Net Worth has her listed at $12 million new worth.

5 Topher Grace

Despite Topher Grace playing the main character, Eric Forman, on the show, he is not the richest actor to come out of the sitcom. Grace earned his break-out role in That '70s Show, but went on to have a successful career. Some roles you might have seen him in are Spider-Man 3, Valentine's Day, BlacKkKlansman and more. He is currently a main role in the show Home Economics. According to Forbes, Grace's net worth is said to be about $14 million.

4 Wilmer Valderrama

Wilmer Valderrama played Fez on the show. That was his break-out role and skyrocketed him into fame. He starred in other films and shows such as Zoom, Unaccompanied Minors, Yo Momma, Handy Manny, NCIS and more. Celebrity Net Worth estimates him to be worth about $18 to $20 million. Currently starring on NCIS, Valderrama earns $100,000 an episode. He also earns some of his income from his music career, where he used the name Eduardo Fresco.

3 Tommy Chong

Tommy Chong starred as Leo Chingkwake, owner of the Foto Hut, on That '70s Show. Being an actor and comedian and one half of Cheech and Chong, he has earned an impressive amount of money. Chong has starred in many roles including Cheech and Chong movies, Zootopia, The Masked Singer, Dancing With The Stars and more. His movie Up In Smoke earned $44 million in the box office. Chong has also earned some of his net worth from his music career. According to Celebrity Net Worth, he is estimated to be worth $20 million.

2 Mila Kunis

Mila Kunis had starred in some small roles in the '90s before making her breakout role as Jackie Burkhart in That '70s Show. Since the show ended, she has starred in other roles such as Family Guy, Friends With Benefits, Bad Moms and its sequel, Ted, Oz the Great and Powerful, Black Swan and more. Family Guy has made her millions and made $225,000 per episode, making $4.5 million per year. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Kunis is worth about $75 million today. Along with her husband, Ashton Kutcher, whom she met on the show, they are living large.

1 Ashton Kutcher

Ashton Kutcher got his break-out role as Michael Kelso on That '70s Show. From there, he went on to be one of the more successful actors on the show starring in many shows and films such as Dude, Where's My Car?, Cheaper By The Dozen, Punk'd, Valentine's Day, New Year's Eve, Two and a Half Men and more. From many years in the entertainment business, Kutcher has racked up an impressive net worth.

He is estimated to be worth about $200 million, Celebrity Net Worth reports. Kutcher earned $800,000 per episode for Two and a Half Men, making $20 million per season, making him the highest paid actor on TV in 2012, according to Forbes. This makes him the richest actor from the cast of That '70s Show.
