Bow Wow Responds To Timbalands Comments That He Doesnt Have Enough Hit Songs To Be On Verzuz

Publish date: 2024-07-20

According to Timbaland, Bow Wow‘s career might not have been as illustrious as the former child star has claimed. The notorious producer recently took to his social media to suggest that Bow Wow didn’t have enough hits to be on Verzuz.

Hot New Hip Hop said today that Bow Wow has commented on the Verzuz battles, arguing he’s not overly interested in them even though fans want to see him on one. Bow Wow wrote on his account in response to some of the requests: “NOT GONNA HAPPEN.” “I don’t want to battle,” the artist wrote.

In case you missed it, fans had asked him to go up against Fabolous, which he had initially agreed too, however, now, he seems uninterested. The organizer of Verzuz, Timbaland, took to his account to say Bow Wow didn’t even have enough hits to go on it.

Timbaland wrote on his account that Bow Wow might not even have “20 in the bag.” Obviously, Bow Wow didn’t appreciate it because he took the time to respond. Bow Wow said to him in response, “you been drinking too much Ciroc.”
