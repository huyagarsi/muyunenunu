What is Carol Castro's Net Worth?

Carol Castro is a Brazilian actress and model who has a net worth of $5 million. Carolina Osorio de Castro was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil in March 1984. Her film debut came in the 2003 movie The Middle of the World. Carol's TV debut was in the series Mulheres Apaixonadas in 2003. In 2004 she starred in the films Summer Show and Peligrosa obsesion. From 2004 to 2005 Castro starred as Angelica on the TV series Senhora do Destino. From 2005 to 2006 she starred as Mercedita Bolivar on Bang Bang.

From 2006 to 2007 she starred as Ruth on the series O Profeta. Castro starred as Sheila in the series Beleza Pura in 2008 and as Mariana in Escrito nas Estrelas in 2010. She has also had recurring roles in the series Morde & Assopra and Amor Eterno Amor. In 2013 she starred as Monica in the movie Trap.com and in 2013 she starred as Sonia in Open Road and Mariana in O Concurso. In August 2008 she appeared in the Brazilian issue of Playboy magazine. In 2013 she won the Brazilian version of Dancing with the Stars called Danca dos Famoso.
