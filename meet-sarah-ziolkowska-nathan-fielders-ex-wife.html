The duo hooked up before Nathan became famous and their union couldn’t survive the demands that came with fame. Thus, they separated in 2014 and their divorce was finalized in 2015. Sarah has since left the limelight and according to reports, she is still doing her job as a librarian.

What is Sarah Ziolkowska’s Nationality?

Going by what we could sieve from existing records, Sarah Ziolkowska was born in Canada which points to her Canadian nationality but she is said to be of Caucasian ethnicity. She was born Sarah Joy Ziolkowska, however, her actual date of birth has never been disclosed. Nevertheless, there are sources that claim she was born in the early 1980s.

Sarah is rather too taciturn with information about her early life and family background, thus, her parents and siblings have never been mentioned in public space. The reading expert spent a better part of her growing-up years in Toronto before migrating to the United States.

Sarah’s Academic History

Growing up in Toronto, Sarah Ziolkowska acquired her primary and secondary school education in the locality. However, when the time came for her to go for higher academic qualifications, she favored the University of Toronto. Sarah joined the institution of higher learning in 2002, graduating in 2007 with a B.A.Sc. (Bachelor of Applied Science) in Psychology .

Obviously not satisfied with a first degree, the Canadian went for an M.L.I.S. (Master of Library & Information Science) at Dalhousie University, Nova Scotia Canada. She started in 2007 and received her honor in 2009.

Six years down the line, after she had worked for a while, Sarah Ziolkowska still went for further academic accolades – in July 2015, the United Latino Students Association bestowed her with the Wilson Reading Systems Certification.

Sarah Ziolkowska Became Nathan Fielder’s Wife in 2007

There are a couple of conflicting accounts about how and when Sarah Ziolkowska first crossed paths with Nathan Fielder, who was just a budding actor and comedian when they fell in love.

The first account said the couple met in Sarah’s home country, Canada, in the mid 2000s and dated for a while before exchanging wedding vows in 2007. However, the second version never revealed where and when they wedded but it said they met in 2007.

Whichever be the case, the couple did exchange wedding vows and lived together as man and wife for some years. Their wedding ceremony was a private affair with a few friends and family members in attendance.

With that said, it is important to note that Sarah and Nathan didn’t have any children during their time together.

What Happened To Sarah Ziolkowska and Nathan Fielder?

The couple’s union couldn’t stand the test of time as they went their separate ways within a short while. It happened that Nathan Fielder who was just a rising star when they met suddenly became famous with the advent of 2013. This happened after he became the co-creator of a comedy reality show “Nathan for You” which he also starred in.

With fame, things started taking a new turn for the couple. Nathan started spending more time at work because of the increased workload and according to Sarah Ziolkowska, he started displaying rude and egoistic behavior that became so uncontrollable that she found it difficult to handle and had to give their marriage a rethink.

They became officially separated in 2014 and their divorce was finalized on the 16th of April 2015 at the Los Angeles-based Stanley Mosk Courthouse.

Nathan, who was initially silent about the whole divorce process, decided to break his silence in 2015. Talking to AV Club publication in an interview session, the American comedian described his divorce as so shocking. He added that the occasions following the separation and divorce were so overwhelming that it resembled the disease, Cancer, Nathan said.

Sarah Ziolkowska’s Career Pursuits

With her track record in education, Sarah decided to forge a career in the education sector – libraries, schools, and students are her major focus. Her work is all bout assisting the younger generation in understanding the theories of digital literacy.

Listed as a librarian, bookkeeper, essayist, custodian, perusing expert, and reading specialist, Sarah Ziolkowska accomplished her internship at W.K Kellogg Health Sciences Library; this was a prerequisite for the award of her B.A.Sc. She was at W.K Kellogg for a period of one year from 2008 to 2009, serving the faculty and the students in fields such as references, documentation, creating webcasts, delivery, and circulation.

Sarah was not idle while she was doing her master’s at Dalhousie; the librarian got busy in the institution of higher learning rendering her services as a teaching assistant.

She is Currently Working as a Librarian cum Reading Specialist

At the moment, Sarah Ziolkowska is an employee of the Culver City-based Park Century School. She works in the position of librarian cum reading specialist. Her job at the school kick-started in October 2012 and as a reading specialist, her job description includes providing assessment and leadership for the school’s reading program.

She is also involved in one-on-one interaction with the students, designing special interventions according to the needs of each student. In addition to her major job function at Park Century School, the reading specialist also undertakes tutorials on a weekly basis; she teaches library classes.

Sarah is equally involved with Halifax Public Library close by. She played a key role at the library during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Where is Sarah Ziolkowska Now?

After their divorce, Sarah Ziolkowska wasted no time in leaving the limelight and went back to her former quiet life. She is still a resident of Culver City, CA, United States, and works at Park Century School in California as a librarian cum reading specialist.

While we are yet to see any report linking the librarian to a man, her ex, Nathan has been linked to quite a few ladies. Notable among them was Maci with whom he had an onscreen romance on the set of the reality show, Nathan For You. However, the comedian has never bothered to confirm or refute the claims.
