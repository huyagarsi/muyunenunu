What is Channing Frye's Net Worth?

Channing Frye is an American professional basketball player who has a net worth of $10 million. Channing Frye was born in White Plains, New York in May 1983. He is a 6'11" power forward and center who played at St. Mary's High School where he was a McDonald's All-American. Frye played his college basketball at the University of Arizona where he was named to the Pac-10 All-Freshman Team and was a two time First-team All-Pac-10 selection. He was drafted #8 by the New York Knicks in the 2005 NBA Draft and played for New York from 2005 to 2007.

Frye played for the Portland Trail Blazers from 2007 to 2009 and for the Phoenix Suns from 2009 to 2014. He played for the Orlando Magic from 2014 to 2016 and has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers since 2016. Frye was named to the NBA All-Rookie First Team in 2006. In 2009 he signed a two year deal with the Suns for $3.8 million and in 2010 he signed a five year deal for $30 million. Frye signed a four year deal with Orlando in 2014 for $32 million. In 2016 he made his first NBA Finals with the Cavaliers.
