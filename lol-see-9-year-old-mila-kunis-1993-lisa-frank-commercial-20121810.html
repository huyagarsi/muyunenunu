LOL! See 9-Year-Old Mila Kunis' 1993 Lisa Frank Commercial

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Long before she was the sexiest woman alive, Mila Kunis was just an adorable stationery enthusiast.

The Ted actress, now 29, starred in a commercial for Lisa Frank's beloved unicorn-and-teddy-bear-covered line of stickers, notebooks, binders and folders back in 1993.

Rocking a matching denim dress and bucket hat, Ashton Kutcher's future girlfriend goes on a shopping spree at a Lisa Frank store. "I gotta have it — what can I say?" she exclaims.

Her feigned excitement over neon school supplies must have gotten her noticed by somebody; two years later, the Black Swan scene-stealer landed the role of Susie Grogan on TV horror movie Piranha, as well as a guest starring part on Baywatch.

Kunis says even at the age of 12, she had a hard time finding her Baywatch character believable.

"I played a blind girl who's lost in the forest next to the beach and needs to be saved. It was absurd," Kunis told W magazine last year. "There's a fire, I get saved, and then I go boogie-boarding. I remember thinking, 'Well, if I'm blind, how am I boogie-boarding? No one ever gave me an answer.'"
