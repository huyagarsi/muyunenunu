Clash of Clans December 2023: Root Rider Sneak Peek and Update Preview

Publish date: 2024-07-03

The forthcoming December 2023 upgrade to the Clash of Clans universe! The Root Rider, a strong new Troop set to disrupt your plans, has just been released by Supercell. This huge character has enormous health and wall-smashing abilities, making him ideal for bursting through opposing fortifications. In a nutshell, she serves as a shield for your heroes while cutting through walls. Consider the possibilities! In this article, we look at the Root Rider’s skills, stats, and how to get this game changer from Town Hall 15. The adventure in Clash of Clans is about to get even more interesting! Clash of Clans December 2023: Root Rider Sneak Peek and Update Preview

Root Rider: A Defensive Force

Before we get into the details, let’s see the Root Rider in action. She demolishes defenses with ease, concentrated on her defensive goal, as showed. This prowess raises some eyebrows, with concerns over her possible overpowering status, which appears to render walls obsolete. Some even believe the Root Rider can outperform the Golem. The Super Wall Breaker, with its 12 less housing space, may still be useful.

Root Rider: A Town Hall 15 Game Changer 

The Root Rider, inspired by the environmental theme of Town Hall 16, becomes available at Town Hall 15 after upgrading your Barracks to Level 16. With the introduction of Town Hall 16, the cost and time required to upgrade to Barracks Level 16 have dropped.

Upgrading to Barracks Level 16 now takes only 12 days (instead of 16) and 12M Elixir (instead of 15M). With the requirements reduced, gamers will be able to unlock the Root Rider more easily.

Root Rider Stats

The Root Rider is a Defense specialist with a single-target assault designed for Ground units. Her training needs include 20 staying spaces and a training time of 3 minutes 40 seconds.

While Town Hall 16 allows you to obtain the Root Rider, upgrading to Town Hall 16 is required to fully utilize her abilities.

Level 1 Stats

  • DPS: 210
  • Wall DPS: 10K
  • HP: 7200

Level 2 Stats

  • DPS: 230
  • Wall DPS: 10K
  • HP: 7600
  • Upgrade Time: 12 days
  • Upgrade Cost: 19M Elixir

Level 3 Stats

  • DPS: 250
  • Wall DPS: 10K
  • HP: 8000
  • Upgrade Time: 14 days
  • Upgrade Cost: 20M Elixir

Sneak Peek Countdown: What’s Next?

Supercell has confirmed one more sneak peek for tomorrow before releasing the patch details for the upcoming version. The December update, as well as the much-anticipated debut of Town Hall 16, are expected to be released on Monday or Tuesday. Stay tuned for more interesting advancements in the world of Clash of Clans!
