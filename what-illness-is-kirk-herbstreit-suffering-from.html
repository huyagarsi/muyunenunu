What Illness Is Kirk Herbstreit Suffering From?

Kirk Herbstreit, the renowned ESPN “College GameDay” analyst, is currently not facing any health troubles or illnesses in 2023. However, he did encounter a health scare in April 2022 when he was treated for blood clots in his lungs and ankles.

The 53-year-old sports commentator shared this experience openly with “The Los Angeles Times,” revealing that it all began with a calf ache, which he initially dismissed as a muscle pull. As the pain progressed to the correct part of his chest, accompanied by shortness of breath, he sought the assistance of a heart specialist.

The heart specialist diagnosed him with clots in his lungs, despite finding clear heart arteries. According to Herbstreit, the clots had traveled through his bloodstream, reached his heart, and ultimately affected his lungs. Fortunately, after several days of treatment with blood thinners, he began to feel better.

Despite the positive outcome, Kirk Herbstreit had to take a temporary break from his responsibilities, including sitting out ESPN’s NFL Draft coverage, as a result of his health troubles during that period.
