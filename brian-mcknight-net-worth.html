Brian McKnight Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Brian McKnight is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer with a net worth of $10 million. Brian McKnight is best known for his falsetto voice and wide range.

Over the course of his career, he has received numerous Grammy Award nominations. His self-titled debut album, released in 1992, went on to sell over a million copies. McKnight released 12 more studio albums between 1995 and 2017.

“Back at One,” his most successful album to date, sold over 3 million copies worldwide. Brian has sold over 25 million albums worldwide in total.

Brian paid just under $1.3 million for a home in Chatsworth, California, in 1999. He sold it in August 2019 for just under $1.8 million.

Brian McKnight was revealed to be the alleged victim of a large-scale fraud perpetrated by his former accountant in February 2014. McKnight sued the former associate for failing to pay his taxes for over ten years in a row, resulting in $1.5 million in back taxes and penalties.

Brian claimed at the time that his bank had placed a lien on his house and that the IRS had frozen his music royalty payments due to his financial difficulties.
