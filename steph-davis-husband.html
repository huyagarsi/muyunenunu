Steph Davis Is A Rock Climber

Steph Davis has established herself as one of the best female rock climbers in the world.

She started her career as a climber shortly after dropping law school in 1995.

She went against the wishes of her parents and family to pursue her passion and eventually achieved success after years of dedication.

For the first seven years, she made her living out of her car, which she would drive to the climbing location and bring back.

She made a bed in the backseat and pursued her passion. It was a time when she made only around $6,000 per year in income.

However, things got better with time as she improved her skills. She eventually started making a better living primarily thanks to the sponsorship deals she received from companies like Patagonia, Five Ten, and Cliff Bar.

But after feeling limited in her ways of climbing, the lady started her journey as a freerider in 2004.

Since then, she has climbed El Capitan, Salathe Wall, and other ranges. Steph became the second woman to free-climb El Capitan and the first to free-climb Salathe Wall.
