Tom Holland Takes a Trip to Monaco with BFF Harrison Osterfield!

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Tom Holland and his best friend Harrison Osterfield are enjoying a weekend away!

The 24-year-old Spider-Man actor was seen attending the F1 Grand Prix of Monaco on Sunday (May 23) in Monte-Carlo, Monaco.

For those who don’t know, the guys met while in school and when Tom got the role of Spider-Man, he hired Harrison to be his personal assistant on the first two movies.

Harrison recently had his breakout role in the Netflix series The Irregulars, which was sadly canceled after just one season on the streaming service.

Also joining the guys for the weekend away was their friend Tuwaine Barrett, who also went to school with them!

Tom‘s most recent movie, Cherry, is now streaming on Apple TV+. Make sure to go watch it if you haven’t seen it yet!
