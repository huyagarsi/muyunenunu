Sister of Jersey Shore Star The Situation, Melissa Sorrentino, Says Yes to the Dress

Publish date: 2024-05-26

It’s gonna be one big Sorrentino situation! Jersey Shore star Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino’s younger sister Melissa is the latest lucky lady to try on wedding dresses on the hit TLC series Say Yes to the Dress, in an episode airing on Friday, June 10.

In an exclusive preview clip of the episode, Melissa, her fiancé, her three older brothers and their mom sit down for a family lunch to talk about her upcoming nuptials to fiancé Michael Sussman.

“I’m so excited I’m getting everybody together today,” Melissa says as she, Sussman and her mom walk into the restaurant.

Her older brother Mike is the last to arrive with his trademark grin on his face.

“What kind of wedding do I want my sister to have?” Mike says in a sit-down confessional with Melissa. “In one word, I would like it to be perfect. In another word, beautiful. Magical.”

At the lunch, opinions got rowdy as Melissa’s brothers and mother argued over how the wedding vibe should be.

“Fun,” one brother quips.

“Classy,” their mother says.

Watch the clip above to see how it all went down! Say Yes to the Dress airs at 9:30 p.m. EST.
