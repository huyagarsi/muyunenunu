Biography:

Bryce Dallas Howard was born on March 2, 1981 in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of director Ron Howard and her mother is actress and writer Cheryl Howard.

In 2004, she had her breakthrough in M. Night Shyamalan’s The Village and Lady in the Water in 2006.
