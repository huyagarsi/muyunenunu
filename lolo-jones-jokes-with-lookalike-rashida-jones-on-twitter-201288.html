Lolo Jones Jokes With Lookalike Rashida Jones on Twitter!

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Are Lolo Jones and Rashida Jones on the fast track to BFF-dom?

Their lookalike status isn't lost on either the Olympic hurdler, 30, or the Parks and Recreation star, 36. On Monday, Lolo reached out to her A-list doppelganger on Twitter.

"Thanks @iamrashidajones for running the first race for me so I could rest up . . . They didn't even notice," Lolo tweeted. "I'll take it from here."

Rashida's reply? "You're welcome! I'm super sore though. Maybe you can return the favor and go to this family thing in my place this weekend?" (The Celeste and Jesse Forever actress is the daughter of legendary music producer Quincy Jones and Mod Squad star Peggy Lipton.)
