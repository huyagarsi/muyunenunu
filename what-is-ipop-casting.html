What are iPOP auditions?

IPOP is a five day talent convention hosted in Hollywood biannually. This is a platform for selected models, actors, singers and dancers from across South Africa to take their talent international and build an international career in the arts.

What does iPOP stand for in acting?

iPOP! is short for the International Presentation Of Performers.

What do you do at iPOP?

HOW DOES iPOP WORK? Talent at the iPOP! event spend five busy days immersed in the entertainment industry. They participate in events including Celebrity Bootcamps, in which performers fine-tune their skills with coaches in acting, modeling, singing and dancing.

Where is iPOP?

Los Angeles Approaching it’s 16th year, iPOP! is held in Los Angeles every winter and summer. Performers attend from over 30 different countries. At the event, they work with industry experts, and participate in competitions in front of the many agents and managers who are looking for the stars of tomorrow.

How do you get into iPOP?

In order to be considered for a place at iPOP, artists must first audition and be accepted. iPOP uses this screening process to find what they think are the most promising young artists to attend their events. However, even prospective students who qualify have to pay tuition to attend.
