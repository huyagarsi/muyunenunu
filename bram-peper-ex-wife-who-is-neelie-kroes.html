Bram Peper Ex-Wife: Who Is Neelie Kroes?

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Bram Peper was a Dutch politician who served as the Mayor of Rotterdam from March 1982 to August 1998 and also served as the Minister of the Interior and Kingdom Relations from August 1998 to March 2000.

Bram Peper bagged a Bachelor of Arts in Social Sciences from the University of Amsterdam and also bagged a Master’s degree in Economy and Sociology from the University of Oslo. Bram Peper then proceeded to bag a Ph.D. from the Erasmus University Rotterdam.

Bram Peper Ex-Wife: Who Is Neelie Kroes?

Bram Peper was married to Dutch politician Neelie Kroes. They married from 1991 to 2003.

Neelie Kroes is a retired Dutch politician of the People’s Party for Freedom and Democracy and businessperson who served as European Commissioner from 22 November 2004 to 1 November 2014.

Kroes studied Economics at the Rotterdam School of Economics obtaining a Master of Economics degree.

Kroes was married to a social democratic minister and mayor Bram Peper. She is a confidant of Ayaan Hirsi Ali, known for her criticism of Islam and having a fatwa issued, and persuaded her to switch allegiance from the social democratic PvdA to the VVD.
