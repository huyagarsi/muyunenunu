Adrienne Bailon &quot;Excited&quot; to Have Rob Kardashian Tattoo Removed

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Third time's the charm? Five years after their breakup, Adrienne Bailon is finally getting Rob Kardashian's name removed from her body. The Real talk show host has tried before to have the tattoo erased, but both attempts were unsuccessful. This one, she hopes—using a new technology called the PicoSure Laser—will at last do the trick.

"I have not been in a relationship with Rob for six years, and I've been with my current boyfriend, [Roc Nation's Lenny S.], for five years. I think it's time to remove it," Bailon, 30, tells Us Weekly of the tattoo. "I think it's always been time, but there wasn't a procedure that I was sure would not only remove it properly but also not leave me with a worse situation than I started with." 

The Cheetah Girls alum first got the body art during her relationship with Kardashian, which lasted from 2007 to 2009. He, too, commemorated their romance with some permanent ink—her name on his left ribcage, which she says has since been erased.

"I was genuinely in love with him and thought, 'If he's going to do this, I'm going to do this, too,'" she recalls, noting that they're still "super cool with each other." Afterward, she realized she wasn't a "tattoo girl"—but it was too late. Now, she's finally ready to put the past behind her.

"I made a very bad decision."
