Publish date: 2024-08-31

He’s a real good man, indeed! Tim McGraw cemented his status as one of country music’s hottest hunks with a sexy photo shoot for the new issue of Men’s Health. Though he’s fully clothed on the cover, the pictures inside the magazine reveal his crazy-ripped torso in all its tanned, muscled glory.

One shot from the spread shows the “Southern Girl” crooner, 47, working out shirtless in a parking lot, his eight-pack abs and bulging biceps on full display. He’s wearing cutoff sweats and sneakers, and lifting a kettlebell with one arm.

“I’ll be the first to admit I take it to extremes,” the country superstar told the mag of his intense workouts. “I’m not one of those people who can sorta half-ass something.”

It helps, too, of course, that he quit drinking—no more beer belly for this guy! “When your wife tells you it’s gone too far, that’s a big wake-up call,” he said of how longtime love Faith Hill helped him to stop partying so hard. “That, and realizing you’re gonna lose everything you have. Not monetarily, not career-wise, but family-wise.”
