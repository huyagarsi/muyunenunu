Sweaters are rightfully associated with fall weather, and we love the ones that dress us up in autumnal colors like burnt sienna, mustard yellow and soft taupe. The only issue is that it tends to get repetitive pretty quickly, especially if you’re layering with the same few pieces every time you go out.

We don’t want to put fall colors into such a small box. They can take on a whole new life when you add a little extra creativity into a piece’s design. What if we told you there was a way to wear rainbow that was actually appropriate for the season? Well, we don’t even have to tell you. We can simply show you this sweater instead!

The stripes of this sweater still capture the beauty of fallen leaves with shades of reddish-orange and yellow as standouts, but they add in some other earthy shades to create a fun, multicolor pattern across the torso and part of the sleeves. The long bell sleeves are already lovely, but the dramatically dropped shoulders show off effortless artistry, which is further emphasized by a break in the stripe pattern on the upper arm!

This loose-knit sweater has a wide neckline that you can let hang off one shoulder, and it’s super soft, not scratchy. It’s quickly becoming a shopper favorite because it’s not too light but not so heavy that you’ll feel like it’s dragging you down. If anything, it’s going to lift you up — your style, your mood, your presence!

This sweater is currently available in eight colors. You’ll see a similar color palette for the stripes on most, with a little bit more of a pop against the black version. Your other options are ivory, blue, royal blue, green, grey, khaki and light yellow. Don’t ask Us to choose a favorite — we fall in love too easily!

This is definitely the type of piece that will take your everyday ensembles from basic to breathtaking. Imagine how chic you’ll look wearing it with a dark-wash pair of skinny jeans and pointed-toe slip-ons? Add on a leather tote and throw your hair in a bun with a silky scrunchie and you’ll have strangers begging you for fashion advice. Icon status!
