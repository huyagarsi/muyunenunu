Joe Francis' Girlfriend Abbey Wilson Gives Birth to Twins

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Twice as nice! Joe Francis and his girlfriend, Abbey Wilson, welcomed their first children, twin baby girls, on Tuesday, Oct. 7. 

According to a birth announcement from the couple, the little ones were born in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico. The announcement read: "Proud first time parents Joe Francis and Abbey Wilson are delighted to announce the birth of their two daughters, Alexandria Claire Francis and Athena Olivia Francis." 

Earlier on Tuesday, Francis posted an Instagram photo of himself in blue scrubs and a surgical mask at the hospital. He captioned it, "Here we go!!! Babies are coming now!" 

The Girls Gone Wild creator, 41, and his longtime love, 26, first announced they were expecting back in April. "We both wanted girls and we wanted them to be healthy and free of genetic diseases so we chose to do IVF," Wilson told Us Weekly at the time. "I found out [I was pregnant] shortly after a blood test confirmed the results and called Joe immediately. We were both screaming so loud with excitement from each end of the phone!"
