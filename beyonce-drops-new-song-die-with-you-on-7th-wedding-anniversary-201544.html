Beyonce Drops New Song &quot;Die With You&quot; on 7th Wedding Anniversary

Publish date: 2024-08-10

She never stops! Beyonce surprised her fans (yet again!) when she dropped a new song titled "Die With You" on Saturday, April 4. Fittingly enough, the ballad was released on her and Jay Z's 7th wedding anniversary.

The 33-year-old entertainer posted a 15-second clip of the tune on her official Facebook page. Clad in a black T-shirt and backwards hat, she sang while playing the piano.

"I don't have a reason to be if I can't be with you," she sweetly sings. "I love so I can die with you."

So, where can you find the full version? Beyonce made sure to direct followers to her hubby's new music streaming service Tidal. The rapper, along with Madonna, Rihanna, Kanye West, Nicki Minaj, and more, announced the app in NYC on Mar 30. Once in Tidal, Queen Bey performs the three-minute song in its entirety.

Oh, and who's the cameraman? None other than Jay Z!
